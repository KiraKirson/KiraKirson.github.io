Pleasant Peasant Planner

Pleasant Peasant Planner is a small, playable demo that I created entirely by myself. Made in Unity, in this game you take the role of a new leader forming their kingdom. You must explore your surroundings for materials, give your citizens tasks, and make decisions that will impact your expansion.

As it stands right now, PPP is a cute little demo that I'm proud of for what it is, but there was definitely a lot more I had planned on putting in. Like always, the project needed to be scoped down, but I did account for that, and tried to rank the tasks I needed to complete in a way that would give me a functional product almost every step of the way. Almost. I'm still learning.

Currently the player can:

  • Move the camera
  • Click on buildings to bring up their floating menu
  • Assign and remove a person from their task
  • Create new people
  • Build buildings once their materials have been gathered
  • Follow a tutorial!
